# Taichung — Discount Cards

**Purpose of this file:** confirm whether any discount card is worth buying in Taichung for our plan (Aug 25 morning only, en route Taipei → Tainan).

> **TL;DR — No card. Our Taichung stops are all free. Ticket cost per person: NT$0.** Just tap an EasyCard/iPASS for any bus or short taxi.

---

## Cards available

Taichung MRT day passes and Klook "Taichung MRT" passes exist, but they're irrelevant to us:
- We use **zero ticketed attractions**, so there's no attraction value to capture.
- The MRT Green Line doesn't reach any of our four stops (reached by city bus or taxi).
- The old "first 10 km of city buses free" perk is now **residents-only** (loaded onto locals' cards) — tourists pay from km 0, so budget a few NT$ per bus hop on EasyCard.

## Recommendation

**Buy nothing.** Carry one topped-up **EasyCard/iPASS** (works Taiwan-wide) for buses/taxis. Per-person ticket cost = **NT$0**.

## Sources & confidence

EasyCard Taiwan-wide usage and the residents-only bus-fare change: taiwanobsessed.com, TripAdvisor Taichung forum, fmyly.com. **Medium-high.** Nothing here is price-sensitive since the plan has no paid entries.
