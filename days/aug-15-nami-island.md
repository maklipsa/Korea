# Aug 15 (Sat) — LIBERATION DAY: NAMI ISLAND + GAPYEONG
> Badges: holiday:Liberation Day

National holiday. Get out of the city — nature day with kid-friendly activities.

## Schedule

| Time | Activity | Price |
|------|----------|-------|
| 7:30 | **ITX-Cheongchun** from Yongsan Station → Gapyeong (~60 min, ~6,000 KRW). Book in advance — Liberation Day = peak. |  |
| 8:45 | **[🌳🧒🚡 Nami Island](https://www.google.com/maps/search/Nami+Island+Gapyeong)** — Enter by **ZIP-LINE across the river** (thrilling for kids 9+!). Metasequoia Alley photos, bike rental, Sky Bikes (elevated pedal cars), craft workshops (glass blowing ~30,000 KRW, ceramic painting ~20,000 KRW). Free-roaming ostriches, peacocks, rabbits. Allow ~3.5h with the zipline + a workshop. | Entry ~16,000 KRW + zip ~44,000 KRW |
| 12:30 | Lunch on Nami Island or at Gapyeong Wharf. |  |
| 13:30 | **[🚡🧒 Gangchon Rail Bike](https://www.google.com/maps/search/Gangchon+Rail+Bike+Gapyeong)** — 8km pedal-powered rail cars through scenic river valleys and tunnels. ~30 min taxi from Nami. **Book a 14:00 slot** in advance. ~1h ride + shuttle back. | ~35,000 KRW/2-person car |
| 15:30 | Bus to **[🌳 Garden of Morning Calm](https://www.google.com/maps/search/Garden+of+Morning+Calm+Gapyeong)** (~40 min from Gapyeong; combo shuttle bus runs from Cheongpyeong). Botanical garden, peaceful end to active day. **Heads-up:** the lighting festival that makes it world-famous runs Dec–Mar; in summer it's "just" a pretty garden, not the postcard version. | ~11,000 KRW |
| 17:30 | Train back to Seoul. |  |
| 19:30 | Light dinner near hotel. |  |

**Tight day.** Three big stops + transit = run-from-thing-to-thing pace. If anyone's flagging, the easy cuts are: skip Garden of Morning Calm (off-season anyway), or skip Rail Bike if Nami zipline was enough thrill. Both can be cut last-minute without breaking the schedule.
