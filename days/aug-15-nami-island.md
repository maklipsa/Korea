**Tight day — and it's Liberation Day.** Three big stops spread across a wide area (Nami/Gapyeong → Gimyujeong east → Garden of Morning Calm back west) = run-from-thing-to-thing pace with backtracking. Aug 15 is a national holiday on a **Saturday**, so Gapyeong is one of the busiest day trips in the country: **book the ITX-Cheongchun both ways and the rail-bike slot well ahead**, expect long ferry/zipwire queues at Nami, and buy Nami tickets online. Realistic call: do **two** of the three stops. Easy cuts: skip Garden of Morning Calm (off-season anyway), or skip Rail Bike if the Nami zipline was enough thrill.

**Transport tip:** the **Gapyeong City Tour Bus** (hop-on-hop-off, ~8,000 KRW adult / ~6,000 child) loops Gapyeong Station ↔ Nami ↔ Petite France ↔ Garden of Morning Calm — handy for those three, but it does **not** serve Gimyujeong/Gangchon Rail Park (reach that by Korail from Gapyeong).
