# Passes & Cards — Detailed Breakdown

## City Passes

### Discover Seoul Pass (48-hour) — 69,000 KRW
**Activate: Aug 16 morning** (covers Aug 16-17)

Includes free entry to:
- N Seoul Tower Observatory (16,000 KRW value) — Aug 16
- Lotte World Adventure (~55,000 KRW value) — Aug 17
- Also covers: Alive Museum, Trick Eye Museum, various others

**Value: ~71,000+ KRW** from just Namsan + Lotte World. Worth it.

Buy: Klook, KKday, or Discover Seoul Pass website. Activate by scanning at first attraction.

### Visit Busan Pass (24-hour) — 49,000 KRW
**Activate: Aug 19 morning**

Includes free entry to:
- Blueline Park Beach Train (7,000-11,000 KRW value)
- Busan Tower (12,000 KRW value)
- SEA LIFE Aquarium (29,000 KRW value) — if used Aug 20
- Songdo Cable Car (17,000-22,000 KRW value) — if used
- Spa Land (20,000 KRW value) — if used

**Note:** Sky Capsule typically NOT included (only Beach Train). Buy on Klook or Visit Busan Pass app.

### Taipei Fun Pass Unlimited (2-day) — ~NT$1,700
**Activate: Aug 21 morning** (covers Aug 21-22)

Includes:
- Taipei 101 Observatory (NT$600 value) — Aug 22
- National Palace Museum (NT$350 value) — Aug 22
- 30+ attractions
- Unlimited MRT + bus rides (saves ~NT$100-200/day)
- Some experiences: Pingxi sky lantern, Maokong tea, pineapple cake DIY

**Value: NT$950+ from just Taipei 101 + NPM + transit. Well worth it.**

Buy: Klook, KKday, or Taipei Fun Pass website.

## Transport Passes

### Taiwan HSR Flexible 2-day Pass — NT$2,500
**For foreigners only** (show passport). Any 2 days within 7 days.

Use on:
- Aug 25: Taipei → Taichung (NT$700) + Taichung → Tainan (NT$650) = NT$1,350
- Aug 27: Kaohsiung → Taipei (NT$1,490) = NT$1,490

**Total regular price: NT$2,840. Saves NT$340.** Plus simplicity of unlimited rides on those days.

Buy: Klook, KKday, or at THSR stations (with passport).

### KTX (Korea High-Speed Rail)
- Seoul → Busan: ~59,800 KRW/person, ~2h15
- Book at [letskorail.com](https://www.letskorail.com) or Korail Talk app
- **Book 30 days ahead** — August = peak season, sells out

## Transit Cards

### T-money Card (Korea)
- Buy at any convenience store at Incheon Airport
- Load ~50,000 KRW to start
- Works on: all buses, subways, some taxis, convenience stores
- Small discount per ride vs. single tickets

### EasyCard (Taiwan)
- Buy at any convenience store or MRT station at Taoyuan Airport
- Load ~NT$500 to start
- Works on: MRT, buses, TRA local trains, convenience stores, YouBike, Maokong Gondola
- 20% discount on MRT rides
